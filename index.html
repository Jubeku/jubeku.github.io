Welcome!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Geophysics at the Institute de Physique du Globe de Paris (IPGP) in France.

In my Ph.D. project I am studying seismic waves generated by rockfalls. In fact, seismic waves can be used to detect, classify and locate events as well as estimate their properties and dynamics. This works not only for rockfalls! Rivers, glaciers, storms, and human activity such as traffic can also be investigated with seismic signals - a study field refered to as Environmental Seismology.

More general, I am enthusiast about data analysis, modeling and visualization. On this website you can find some of my Projects, as well as Codes and Visualizations I have produced.

Finally, feel free to contact me for any sort of reason!
